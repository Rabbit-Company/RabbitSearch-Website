General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR)

At Rabbit Search, we take your privacy very seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information.

Rabbit Search does not collect any personal data or use cookies. Therefore, we do not process any personal data within the meaning of the GDPR. You are free to use our service without providing any personal data. As we do not collect any data or use cookies, we have no access to or control over any personal data you may provide to third-party websites that are linked from our search results. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of these third-party websites before providing them with any personal data.
